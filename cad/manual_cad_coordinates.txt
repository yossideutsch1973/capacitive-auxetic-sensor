MANUAL CAD STEPS:

3. Extrude the 2D profile to 4.0 mm height
4. Create a 3×3 rectangular pattern with 10 mm spacing
5. Add bottom plate: 36×36×0.3 mm at Z=-0.3

7. Add 5 support posts: ⌀1.5×5.5 mm at specified positions
